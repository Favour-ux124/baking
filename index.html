• About Us

    Welcome to Bake My Day

    Bake My Day is a catering company owned by favour, established to provide an array of Nigerian finger foods to help spice your memorable events and also to satisfy your cravings, we create traditional finger foods like: spring rolls, fried Dough(puff puff), Donut, Samosa, Sausage roll,Egg roll, Sharwarma, Meat pie, fish roll, meat roll, Chin-chin, peppered Goat, turkey, chicken, beef and snail with a blend of African prices to create our unique taste. we also make fresh fruit juices, cakes, we're passionate about baking and making your day a little sweeter.

    Our vision

    our vision is to be the preferred choice for all finger foods, drinks, cakes to help make your events memorable and special and to satisfy your cravings, our mission is to provide the highest quality baked goods, made with love and care, to our customers. At Bake My Day, we're committed to excellence in everything we do. from our customer service to our baking, we strive to be the best and make every customer feel special.
